    Welcome to Khan Roulette V2.0 . The game rules are simple. 3 doors will appear and to win the game you must open all 3 without opening a red door. If, you hit a red door the game will automatically restart.

    Unlike V1.0 the doors don't have preset configurations. I raised an unruly child this time around. Everytime you press a door it will truly open a random color.

    By default, there is a countdown that will begin during the first round. If, the countdown reaches 0 it will automatically make you lose. Try to beat my score of 8 before time runs out (:

  • 4

    If, you don't like the pressure just avoid clicking the countdown and it won't start counting down while you play. Press the eliptical click me button when you'd like to begin (:
