    Welcome to Khan Roulette. The game rules are simple. 3 doors will appear and to win the game you must open all 3 doors without opening a red door. If, you hit a red door the game will end and automatically restart.

    The doors don't have preset configurations. So don't try to find patterns in which colors appear when the doors are opened.

    Try to get a score of 4 before time runs out (: If, you want more pressure just click the timerand it will count down to 0 while you play. If, the timer reaches 0 you lose and your streak is reset.

    Once you feel comfortable with the instructions and are ready to play. Press 'Click For Game' to reveal the game.
